About Me

Welcome to my world of innovation, creativity, and impactful problem-solving! I'm Shahar Maoz, a versatile professional combining over 15 years of experience in education, music, and performance with a deep passion for technology and software development.

A Journey of Transformation

My career began in music and education, where I honed my skills as a mentor, performer, and event organizer. Over time, I discovered a love for technology, leading me to transition into the dynamic field of software development. This shift allowed me to merge my creative mindset with technical expertise, crafting solutions that not only work seamlessly but also resonate with users on a human level.

What I Do

  • Frontend Development: Crafting intuitive and engaging user interfaces using React, JavaScript, and TypeScript.
  • Backend Engineering: Building robust, scalable server-side solutions with Node.js, Python, and SQL.
  • Automation and Optimization: Developing custom Python tools for workflow automation, data retrieval, and social media integration.
  • DevOps and Platforms: Utilizing Docker and other tools to streamline deployment and collaboration.

Beyond Coding

Highlights from my career include designing financial management tools for educators and performers, creating interactive learning applications, and developing inclusive music programs for special needs communities. My work combines technical precision with human connection, ensuring impactful results.

Collaboration is Key

I value collaboration and have worked with educators, therapists, and developers to achieve shared goals while fostering creativity and innovation.
